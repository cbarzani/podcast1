## 📋 How to Connect chirokyan.net Domain

### Option 1: Using Netlify (FREE - RECOMMENDED ⭐)

#### Step 1: Deploy to Netlify
1. Go to [netlify.com](https://netlify.com)
2. Sign up with GitHub/GitLab/Email
3. Click "Add new site" → "Deploy manually"
4. Download your website files from Figma Make
5. Drag and drop the folder to Netlify

#### Step 2: Connect Your Domain
1. In Netlify, go to **Site Settings** → **Domain Management**
2. Click **Add custom domain**
3. Enter: `chirokyan.net`
4. Netlify will show you DNS records to add

#### Step 3: Update DNS at Your Domain Registrar
Go to where you bought `chirokyan.net` (Namecheap, GoDaddy, etc.) and add these DNS records:

**Option A: Use Netlify Nameservers (Easiest)**
```
ns1.netlify.com
ns2.netlify.com
ns3.netlify.com
ns4.netlify.com
```

**Option B: Use A Record + CNAME**
```
Type: A
Name: @
Value: 75.2.60.5

Type: CNAME
Name: www
Value: [your-site-name].netlify.app
```

## 🔍 How to Get Your Website on Google Search

### Step 1: Submit to Google Search Console

1. **Go to Google Search Console**
   - Visit: [search.google.com/search-console](https://search.google.com/search-console)
   - Sign in with your Google account

2. **Add Your Website**
   - Click "Add Property"
   - Enter: `https://chirokyan.net`
   - Choose "URL prefix" method

3. **Verify Ownership**
   - Download the HTML file Google gives you
   - Upload it to your website root folder
   - Click "Verify"

4. **Submit Your Sitemap**
   - I'll create a sitemap for you (see below)
   - In Search Console, go to "Sitemaps"
   - Submit: `https://chirokyan.net/sitemap.xml`

### Step 2: Submit to Bing
1. Go to [bing.com/webmasters](https://www.bing.com/webmasters)
2. Add `chirokyan.net`
3. Import from Google Search Console (easier!)
